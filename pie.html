Grandma's lemon Maringa Pie


    one 9-inch pie
    30 Min - prep time
    10 min - cook time
    40 Min - Total
    8 Serving

INGREDIENTS

    1 cup white sugar
    2 tbsp all purpose flour
    3 tbsp cornstarch
    1/4 tbsp salt
    1 cup water
    2 lemons,juised and zested
    4 tbsp butter
    6 egg yolks
    1 (9-inch) pie crust
    6 eggs whites
    6 tbsp white sugar

DIRECTION

  1. whisk together i cup sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt
  2. stir in water , lemon,and lemon zest
  3. cook over medium-high heat ,untilmixture comes to a boil
  4. place eggs yolks in a bowl and whisk in 1/2 cup sugar
  5. bring to a boil and continu to cook
  6. whisk together egg and the remaining sugar
  7. remove from heat
  8. pour filling into pasty shell
  9. Make Meringue:In large glass or metal bowl
    • whip egg whites until foamy.
    • Add sugar gradually until stiff peaks foamy.
    • spead Meringue over pie , sealing the egdge at the crust.
  10. Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until Meringue is golden brown
